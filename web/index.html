Sanjay Gandhi Polytechnic !

Sanjay Gandhi Polytechnic, bellary was the first premier private co-educational Polytechnic established in the year 1991 to provide quality technical education, affording young men and women the opportunity to equip themselves for employment. The Institute is recognised by the Government of Karnataka, the All India Council for Technical Educatio(AICTE), New Delhi, and is affiliated to Directorate of Technical Education Bangalore. Our Mission To inculcate moral and social vales in students and to provide qualoity technical education to mee the requirement of the changing industrial scenario. Our Vision To be at the forefront of technical education by training quality manpower for the industry and society at large through demand driven, social and environment conscious education.
